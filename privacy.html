Privacy Policy for Write Below Pro

Last Updated: May 2026

Write Below Pro respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how the app handles information.

Information We Collect

Write Below Pro does not collect, store, transmit, or share personal information.

The app allows users to select photos from their device, add text below the photo, and save the resulting image. All processing occurs locally on the user's device.

Photos and Files

When you choose a photo, the app accesses only the photo you select. Photos are processed locally on your device and are not uploaded to any server.

Images created by the app are saved only to locations you choose on your device.

Personal Information

Write Below Pro does not require user accounts and does not collect:

  • Names
  • Email addresses
  • Phone numbers
  • Physical addresses
  • Payment information
  • Government identification numbers

Data Sharing

Write Below Pro does not sell, rent, share, or disclose user data to third parties.

Children's Privacy

Write Below Pro is not directed toward children under the age of 13. The app does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Security

Because all processing occurs locally on the user's device, information remains under the user's control.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any updates will be posted on this page.
